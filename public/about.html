About

Get a taste of the ups & downs of the stock market with our web app in a quirky, risk-free environment using REAL stock market data!.

Learn how to play!

How to Play

  • You will be presented with a series of charts of various stock tickers

  • Your challenge is to select whether to buy, sell or hold the stock for an unknown period of time

  • After making your selection, the chart will automatically update to show you the result

  • Select "Next Game" to load the next chart with the next ticker
